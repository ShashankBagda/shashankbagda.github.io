About

Computer Software & Hardware Engineer

I’m Shashank Bagda — a technology enthusiast, creative thinker, and aspiring researcher dedicated to shaping the future of intelligent systems. Fueled by curiosity and purpose, I specialize in bridging the realms of hardware and software to develop impactful solutions that matter.

Currently pursuing my B.Tech in Information and Communication Technology at Marwadi University, I have actively immersed myself in cutting-edge research and innovation. My journey includes back-to-back research internships at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati, where I explored advanced topics such as Multi-Network Architectures for Deep Learning, In-Memory Computing, and Hardware Trojan Detection — leveraging simulators like GEM5 and SIAM to optimize computational performance in modern architectures.

Beyond the lab, I lead initiatives that empower and inspire. As the founder of the Circuitology Club — one of the most dynamic technical communities on campus — and Chair of the IEEE MEFGI Student Branch, I’ve organized 20+ events, mentored peers, secured funding, and driven hands-on STEM engagement across institutions.

My portfolio is a fusion of embedded intelligence and digital systems from building AI-powered handwriting-to-G-code converters and gesture-controlled vehicles to developing IoT dashboards and secure network frameworks. I work fluently across Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, Verilog, Python, Java, and C++, turning abstract concepts into working prototypes.

I believe innovation begins with intention. Whether it’s designing next-gen chip architectures or simplifying engineering workflows with automation, I’m driven by a desire to solve problems that create real-world impact — combining logic with creativity, structure with imagination.

If you’re looking for someone who doesn’t just follow trends but defines them — let’s build the future together.
