ABOUT VASTOME

We build software
that matters.

Vastome is a technology engineering firm specialising in cloud infrastructure, AI systems, and SaaS product development for enterprise clients.

OUR MISSION

We exist to help ambitious companies build technology they can be proud of — robust, scalable, and built to last.

Too many engineering projects end up as technical debt. We work differently: every engagement starts with deep discovery, every architecture decision is documented and justified, and every line of code is built to be maintained. We're not trying to be the biggest engineering firm. We're trying to be the one our clients recommend.

Why clients stay

We optimise for recommendation, not volume — with documented decisions, resilient systems, and delivery that stands up after launch.

Long-term maintainability is a product outcome, not a clean-up phase.

A BRIEF HISTORY

Steady growth, deliberate expansion.

2018

Founded with a single client and a belief that enterprise software didn't have to be slow, expensive, or miserable.

2020

Expanded into AI/ML engineering as demand for production-grade ML systems outgrew the market's supply.

2022

Launched our SaaS practice, helping B2B startups and mid-market companies build multi-tenant platforms.

2024

Named one of the top engineering partners by three consecutive clients. Now a team of 40+.
