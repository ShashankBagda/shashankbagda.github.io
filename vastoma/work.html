Migration delivery

Challenge

ClearStream Bank was running a 15-year-old on-premises core banking system. Infrastructure costs were exceeding $4M annually, payment processing latency averaged 4 seconds, and the engineering team spent 60% of their time on maintenance rather than product development. The bank needed to modernize without disrupting service to their 2 million customers.

Approach

Vastoma's cloud architects designed a strangler-fig migration pattern, allowing new services to be built on AWS while legacy components were progressively decommissioned. We introduced asynchronous payment processing with SQS and Lambda, Redis caching for account lookups, and Aurora PostgreSQL for the core ledger. A blue-green deployment strategy ensured zero downtime throughout the 16-week migration.

Outcome

"Vastoma delivered what three previous vendors failed to: a cloud migration that actually shipped on time and under budget. The improvement in payment latency alone has driven measurable customer satisfaction gains." â€” VP Engineering, ClearStream Bank
